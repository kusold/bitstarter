Going on a solo bike ride can wear you down. There is no one to talk to or even race against.

Tell Bike Chain when, where, and how you want to ride, and we try to match you against other people that want a similar ride. Gone are the days of grueling hill climbs with no one motivating you. Gone are the days of silent city cruises. Gone are the days of risky solo mountain bike rides.

FAQ

What is BikeChain?
BikeChain is an app to help you find other people to ride your bike with regardless of your riding style.
How does BikeChain work?
Simply enter your ideal ride, and an anonymous post appears on our site. People can then ask if they can join you for your ride. If you accept their request, then we exchange twitter handles so that you can further organize the meetup.
